Initial Setup (Onboarding)

Learn how to set up your Ambrosia Point of Sale system for the first time through the guided setup wizard.

Overview​

The first time you launch Ambrosia, you'll be greeted by a setup wizard that walks you through the essential configuration steps. This onboarding process ensures your POS system is properly configured with your business information, admin account, and preferences before you start processing sales.

The setup wizard consists of 4 main steps:

  1. Select your business type
  2. Create your admin account
  3. Enter your business details
  4. Review and confirm your setup

What You'll Need:

  • Your business information (name, address, phone, email)
  • Your tax ID (RFC) if applicable
  • A business logo image (optional but recommended)
  • A secure password for your admin account

Step 1: Select Your Business Type​

The first screen asks you to identify what type of business you're running. This selection helps Ambrosia tailor the interface and features to best suit your needs.

Available Business Types​

Store

  • Recommended for: Retail shops, boutiques, hardware stores, electronics shops, Bitcoin merchandise stores
  • Focus: Product-based sales with inventory management
  • Features optimized for: SKU tracking, stock management, product categories

Restaurant

  • Recommended for: Restaurants, cafes, food trucks, bars, coffee shops
  • Focus: Food service and hospitality
  • Features optimized for: Table management, order modifications, kitchen workflow

Making Your Selection​

  1. Review the two business type options
  2. Click on the option that best describes your business
  3. The selected option will be highlighted
  4. Click the Next button to continue

Step 2: Create Your Admin Account​

This step creates the primary administrator account for your POS system. This account will have full access to all features and settings.

Required Fields​

User Name​

  • Enter your full name or the name of the primary administrator
  • This will appear in the system and on reports
  • Example: John Smith, Maria Garcia, Admin
  • Can be changed later in the Users section

PIN (4-digit)​

  • Create a secure 4-digit PIN code
  • This PIN allows quick access to the system during daily operations
  • Will be used every time you log in
  • Example: 1234, 7890, 4582

Important: Choose a PIN that's:

  • Easy for you to remember
  • Not obvious (avoid 0000, 1234)
  • Unique to this account
  • Kept confidential

Password​

  • Create a strong password for your admin account
  • This provides an additional layer of security
  • Used for accessing sensitive settings and features
  • Used to access your wallet

Password Requirements:

  • Minimum 8 characters
  • Must include at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • Must include at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • Must include at least one number (0-9)
  • Must include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

Password Example: Ambrosia2025!

Password Strength Indicator​

Below the password field, you'll see a guideline reminder showing the requirements. Make sure your password meets all criteria before proceeding.

Completing This Step​

  1. Enter your name in the User Name field
  2. Create and enter a 4-digit PIN in the PIN field
  3. Create and enter a strong password in the Password field
  4. Verify all information is correct
  5. Click the Next button to continue

Step 3: Enter Your Business Details​

This step collects important information about your business that will appear on receipts, reports, and throughout the system.

Required Fields​

Store Name​

  • Enter your official business name
  • This appears on receipts and customer-facing materials
  • Example: Bitcoin Coffee Shop, Lightning Electronics, Jade Hardware Store

Address​

  • Enter your complete business address
  • Include street, number, city, state, and postal code
  • Example: 123 Main Street, Guadalajara, Jalisco, 44100
  • Used for receipts and official documentation

Phone​

  • Enter your business contact phone number
  • Include country code if applicable
  • Example: +52 33 1234 5678, (555) 123-4567
  • Customers may use this to contact you

Email​

  • Enter your business email address
  • Used for system notifications and customer contact
  • Example: contact@yourstore.com, sales@bitcoinshop.com
  • Must be a valid email format

RFC (Tax ID)​

  • Enter your Mexican tax identification code (RFC)
  • Must be exactly 13 characters
  • Format: 4 letters + 6 numbers + 3 alphanumeric characters
  • Example: XAXX010101000
  • Required for tax compliance and official receipts

Currency​

  • Select your primary business currency
  • Default: MXN (Mexican Peso)
  • This is the currency displayed in prices throughout the system
  • Bitcoin prices convert automatically based on this currency

Available Currencies:

  • MXN (Mexican Peso) - Default
  • USD (US Dollar)
  • EUR (Euro)
  • Other currencies may be available depending on your configuration

Store Logo (Optional)​

  • Upload an image file representing your business
  • This logo appears on receipts and in the interface
  • Creates a professional appearance

Logo Requirements:

  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, or GIF
  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • Recommended size: 500x500 pixels (square format works best)
  • Transparent background (PNG) recommended for best results

Uploading Your Logo:

  1. Click the Upload button or image placeholder
  2. Select your logo file from your computer
  3. The image preview will appear
  4. You can click again to change the image if needed

Completing This Step​

  1. Fill in all required fields:
    • Store name
    • Address
    • Phone
    • Email
    • RFC
    • Currency
  2. (Optional) Upload your store logo
  3. Review all information for accuracy
  4. Click the Next button to continue

Important: Double-check your RFC as it's critical for tax compliance.

Step 4: Review Your Setup Summary​

This final step shows you all the information you've entered, allowing you to verify everything is correct before completing the setup.

What You'll See​

The summary screen displays all your configuration in organized sections:

Business Type

  • Shows the type you selected (Store or Restaurant)

Admin Account

  • Username: The administrator name you created
  • Password: Hidden for security (shown as dots)

Business Details

  • Store name
  • Address
  • Phone
  • Email
  • RFC
  • Currency
  • Store logo (if uploaded)

Making Changes​

If you spot any errors or need to change something:

  1. Look for the pen icon next to each section
  2. Click the pen icon for the section you want to edit
  3. You'll be taken back to that step
  4. Make your corrections
  5. Click Next to return to the summary

Completing the Setup​

Once you've verified all information is correct:

  1. Review each section carefully
  2. Confirm your admin password is secure
  3. Verify your RFC is correct (very important!)
  4. Check that your contact information is accurate
  5. Click the Finish button to complete the setup

Setup Complete!​

Congratulations! You've successfully configured your Ambrosia Point of Sale system.

What Happens Next​

After completing the setup wizard:

  1. The system saves your configuration
  2. Your database is initialized with your business information
  3. Your admin account is created
  4. You're redirected to the login screen

First Login​

Now it's time to access your system for the first time.

Login Steps:

  1. Select Your User

    • You'll see a list with your admin user
    • Click on your username to select it
    • This is the account you just created

  2. Enter Your PIN

    • A PIN pad will appear
    • Enter the 4-digit PIN you created during setup
    • Make sure to enter it correctly

  3. Sign In

    • Click the Sign In button
    • The system will verify your credentials
    • You'll be authenticated and logged in

First Time in the Dashboard​

After successful login, you'll arrive at the Dashboard - the main control panel of Ambrosia.

What You'll See:

  • Users: Shows your admin account (1 user)
  • Products: Empty (0 products) - you'll add these next
  • Sales: Empty (0 sales) - will populate as you process transactions

Navigation Menu (left sidebar):

  • Users - Manage staff accounts
  • Products - Add and manage inventory
  • Cart - Process sales (POS interface)
  • Orders - View transaction history
  • Wallet - Manage Lightning Network funds
  • Settings - Configure system preferences
