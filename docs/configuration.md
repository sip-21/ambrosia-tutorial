# Initial Setup (Onboarding)

The first time you open the application, a setup wizard will guide you through the process of configuring your Point of Sale system. This onboarding consists of 4 steps: business type, admin account creation, business details, and a final summary.

## 1. Select your business type
Choose the type of business you are setting up. This helps personalize the interface and available features.

Available options:
- **Store** - A retail business selling products directly to consumers.
- **Restaurant** - A food service business preparing and serving meals to customers.

After selecting the appropriate option, click **Next**.

## 2. Create your admin account
This will be the main account you use to access the PoS system.

Fill in the following fields:
- **User Name** – Primary administrator name.
- **PIN** – Short code for quick access.
- **Password** – Secure password for your account.

A password guideline is shown below:
It must contain at least 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Click **Next** to continue.

## 3. Enter your business details
Provide your business information:
- **Store name** – Commercial name.
- **Address** – Physical address of your location.
- **Phone** – Contact phone number.
- **Email** – Business email address.
- **RFC** – Tax ID code (13 characters).
- **Currency** – Main currency for transactions.
- **Store logo** – Upload a PNG, JPG, or GIF file (max 5MB).

Once everything is filled out, click **Next**.

## 4. Review your setup summary
Before completing the setup, verify that all information is correct.

You will see a breakdown of:
- Business Type
- Admin Account (username and password)
- Business Details (full business info)

If you need to make changes, click the pen icon in the relevant section.

Click **Finish** to complete the setup.


## Setup complete!

Once completed, your Point of Sale system is fully configured and ready to start adding products, processing sales, and managing daily operations.
